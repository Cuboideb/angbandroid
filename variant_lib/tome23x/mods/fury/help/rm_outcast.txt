#####R=== Outcasts ===

#####GDescription
Outcasts are hated and cast out by all free races for various reasons.
They are unlucky, but are strong and smart.

#####GStat Modifiers
Strength       +4
Intelligence   +4   
Wisdom         +0 
Dexterity      +3  
Constitution   +4 
Charisma       -999     
Hit Dice       +3 sides        
Exp Penalty    +0%

#####GStarting Equipment
An Outcast character begins the game with:
    Some rations
    Some torches
