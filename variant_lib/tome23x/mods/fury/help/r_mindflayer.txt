#####R=== Mindflayers ===

#####GDescription
Mindflayers are a race of humanoid creatures who live underground.  They
are very intelligent and wise, but not very strong or tough.  They make
excellent magicians or mindcrafters.  However, they look odd, with 
octopus-like heads.
