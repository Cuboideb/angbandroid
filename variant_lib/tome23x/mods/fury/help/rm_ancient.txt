#####R=== Ancients ===

#####GDescription
An old member of the race, they have lost the strength of their early 
years and have become scarred, but they have become very wise and 
smart, and their eyes can see what others can't.


#####GStat Modifiers
Strength       -3 
Intelligence   +3   
Wisdom         +5 
Dexterity      -2  
Constitution   -3 
Charisma       -2     
Hit Dice       -3 sides        
Exp Penalty    +10%


#####GStarting Equipment
An Ancient character begins the game with:
    Some rations
    Some torches
