#####R=== Berserkers ===

#####GDescription
Truly fearsome warriors indeed, a Berserker is immune to fear and paralysis.
As they gain levels they will also be able to reflect bolts with their tough 
skin alone. They can also remove cursed equipment by sheer force alone.
