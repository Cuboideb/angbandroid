#####R=== Gimli Race ===

#####GDescription
Gimli is a dwarf, one of the Nine members of the Fellowship of the 
Ring.  He is the son of Gloin, who was one of the thirteen companions
of Thorin Oakenshield, on Bilbo's great adventure.
